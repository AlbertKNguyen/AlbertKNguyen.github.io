Intro

Hello. My name is Albert and am currently a Junior Computer Science and Engineering Student at UC Merced. My hobbies are programming, playing video games, and creating art such as music. I love to do these things as they allow me to express my creativity. Particularly, my interest in programming or Computer Science first started with a fascination with computers. When I built my first computer, I wondered how each component was powered and how a computer knows what to do. I explored these questions with an Arduino microcontroller circuit board and became interested in circuits and programming. By completing many tutorial projects, I learned about circuits and executing code to perform actions in real life. I combined the concepts I learned to create my own projects and made an alarm clock and a digital timer. Seeing the result of my work and realizing the limitless possibilities inspired me to learn more and pursue Computer Science. My curiosity and initiative to learn helped me build a foundation to succeed both in and out of the classroom. I have completed programming classes in Python, Java, C++, Data Structures, Assembly Language, and look forward to taking upper-division classes in the upcoming semesters. My knowledge of multiple programming languages makes me a versatile individual and enables me to help my peers. Additionally, programming has improved my critical thinking skills by encouraging me to find the optimal solution to a given problem. I am prepared and excited to continue expanding my knowledge and skills by obtaining a summer internship.
